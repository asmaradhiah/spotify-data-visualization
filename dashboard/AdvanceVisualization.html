TECHNIQUE 1 · HEAT MAP

Audio Feature Correlation Heat Map

Hover a cell for the exact correlation coefficient. Green = the two traits rise together; red = one rises as the other falls.

💡 Key takeaway: Energetic songs are reliably less acoustic — but no single audio trait predicts how many streams a song gets.

WHY THIS CHART

Comparing nine traits two at a time would take dozens of separate charts. One heat map shows every pairwise relationship at once, so patterns across the whole catalogue are visible in a single glance.

WHAT IT TELLS US

High-energy tracks read as less acoustic and slightly more upbeat. Streams, on the other hand, barely track any single trait — tempo, energy, danceability, none of them reliably predict a hit.

VS. A REGULAR CHART

A normal bar or line chart shows one or two variables at a time. This heat map fits every possible pairing into the same fixed space, and stays just as readable if more traits are added later.

TECHNIQUE 2 · PARALLEL COORDINATES PLOT

Genre Cluster Audio Profile

Each line traces one genre cluster's typical shape across eight audio traits. Drag on any axis to filter; click a legend chip to isolate a cluster; hover a line for details.

🎧 Key takeaway: Every genre cluster has its own audio "fingerprint" — Dance tracks run high-energy and danceable, Acoustic tracks bend the opposite way on those same traits.

WHY THIS CHART

A genre cluster is defined by several traits together, not one. Parallel coordinates keeps all eight traits visible on the same lines, so a cluster's whole "shape" reads at a glance.

WHAT IT TELLS US

Dance and Acoustic clusters sit at opposite ends of the energy/acousticness traits. Switching on individual tracks reveals outliers — songs that don't match their own cluster's usual shape, good candidates for cross-genre playlists.

VS. A REGULAR CHART

A grouped bar chart can only show one trait's average per cluster at a time. This chart shows all eight traits together, and can reveal individual outliers a bar chart's average would hide.

Dataset: Most Streamed Spotify Songs 2023 (cleaned), filtered to released_year 2019–2023 · 769 tracks · Genre Cluster is a heuristic derived from audio features, consistent with the main dashboard page.
